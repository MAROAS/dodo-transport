Drive Long Distance with DODO Travel

Interested in becoming a DODO Travel driver? Long-distance driving can be rewarding and lucrative. Here's what you need:

Driver Requirements

  • Hold a valid unrestricted Australian driver's licence (minimum 2 years experience)
  • Be at least 25 years of age (due to insurance requirements)
  • Be an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or have working rights in Australia
  • Have an ABN and be GST-registered
  • Pass comprehensive background checks (criminal record and driving history)
  • Hold necessary state-specific commercial driving accreditations
  • Maintain appropriate commercial/long-distance insurance coverage
  • Complete DODO long-distance driver training program
  • Have excellent knowledge of Australian highways and regional routes
  • Possess strong customer service and communication skills
  • Be comfortable with overnight stays for interstate trips (accommodations provided)

Vehicle Requirements

  • Vehicle must seat 4-5 passengers comfortably
  • No older than 8 years for long-distance service
  • Must be in excellent mechanical condition - safety is paramount
  • Pass comprehensive vehicle inspections
  • Registered as a commercial passenger vehicle
  • Equipped with proper commercial insurance for interstate travel
  • Air conditioning and heating must be fully functional
  • Large trunk space for passenger luggage
  • GPS navigation system required
  • Emergency equipment (first aid kit, warning triangles, etc.)

Preferred Vehicles for Long Distance

  • Toyota Camry, Corolla (hybrid models preferred)
  • Mazda 6
  • Honda Accord
  • Hyundai Sonata
  • Volkswagen Passat
  • Subaru Outback, Legacy
  • Toyota RAV4, Kluger
  • Mazda CX-5, CX-9

Additional Qualifications (Preferred)

  • Previous experience with long-distance driving or rideshare services
  • Clean driving record with no major violations
  • First aid certification
  • Defensive driving course completion
  • Excellent punctuality and reliability record

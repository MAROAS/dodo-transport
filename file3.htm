Driver requirements in VIC

  • Have a smartphone, mobile number and email address
  • Be an Australian citizen or permanent resident or otherwise have the right to work in Australia
  • Have held an unrestricted Australian driver licence for at least 6 months
  • Have an ABN and be GST-registered
  • Hold a current Driver Accreditation
  • Be listed as the insured driver on the insurance

Vehicle Requirements in VIC

Vehicles must meet the state government’s requirements and must be in great condition, free of any damage and decals. The vehicle must:

  • be no more than 10 years
  • have a minimum of 4 doors
  • seat a minimum of 4 passengers
  • seat a maximum of 6 passengers

RWC accepted. Exemption for new vehicle within 12 months

Vehicles must be covered under a current third-party or comprehensive insurance

Vehicles must be registered as a Commercial Passenger Vehicle in Victoria

Driver requirements in NSW

  • Have a smartphone, mobile number and email address
  • Be an Australian citizen or permanent resident or otherwise have the right to work in Australia

Have held an unrestricted Australian driver licence for at least 12 months

Have an ABN and be GST-registered

Have not had any serious criminal offences

Have not had any serious traffic offences

Be listed as the insured driver on the insurance

Vehicle Requirements in NSW

Vehicles must meet the state government’s requirements and must be in great condition, free of any damage and decals. The vehicle must:

  • be no more than 15 years
  • have a minimum of 4 doors

Vehicles must seat a minimum of 4 passengers

Vehicles must seat a maximum of 6 passengers

Vehicles must not be an ex-taxi

Vehicles must pass a pink slip vehicle inspection in the last 12 months. *Exempt for new vehicle registered in 12 months

Vehicles must be registered in NSW and covered by the NSW CTP insurance

Vehicles must be covered under a current third-party or comprehensive insurance

Vehicles must seat a minimum of 4 passengers

Vehicles must not be an ex-taxi

*Exempt for new vehicle registered within 12 months

Vehicles must be covered by a third-party or comprehensive insurance

Vehicles must hold a Booked Hired Service Licence (BHSL)

Driver requirements in SA

Have a smartphone, mobile number and email address

Be an Australian citizen or permanent resident or otherwise have the right to work in Australia

Have held an unrestricted Australian driver licence for at least 12 months

Have an ABN and be GST-registered

Hold a current Driver Accreditation

Hold a valid Operator Accreditation

Vehicles must not be an ex-taxi

Vehicles must pass a vehicle inspection in the past 12 months

Vehicles must be covered under a current Upgraded CTP insurance (class: 48, 98, 7, 58)

Vehicles must be covered under a current third-party or comprehensive insurance

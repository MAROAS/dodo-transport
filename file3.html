Drive DODO Express - Requirements & Benefits

Interested in driving for DODO Express? Here's everything you need to know:

Driver Requirements

  • Hold a valid unrestricted Australian driver's licence (minimum 12 months)
  • Be at least 21 years of age
  • Be an Australian citizen, permanent resident, or have working rights in Australia
  • Have an ABN and be GST-registered
  • Pass a comprehensive background check (criminal record and driving history)
  • Hold necessary state-specific ride-sharing accreditations
  • Maintain appropriate commercial/rideshare insurance
  • Complete DODO driver training and orientation
  • Have a smartphone (iOS or Android) for the driver app

Vehicle Requirements

  • Vehicle must seat 4-5 passengers (including driver)
  • No older than 10 years (varies by state - check local requirements)
  • Must be in excellent condition - clean interior, no damage or excessive wear
  • Pass all state vehicle inspections and roadworthy certificates
  • Registered as a ride-sharing or commercial passenger vehicle
  • Equipped with proper commercial insurance coverage
  • Air conditioning must be fully functional
  • Minimum 4 doors for passenger access
  • No commercial branding or taxi signage

Eligible Vehicle Examples

  • Toyota Camry, Corolla
  • Mazda 3, 6
  • Honda Civic, Accord
  • Hyundai i30, Elantra, Sonata
  • Volkswagen Golf, Passat
  • Ford Focus, Mondeo
  • Nissan Altima, Maxima
  • Subaru Impreza, Legacy

State-Specific Requirements

Requirements vary by state. Click below to view detailed requirements for your state:

  • VIC: Minimum 6 months unrestricted licence, Driver Accreditation required
  • NSW: Minimum 12 months unrestricted licence, no serious offences
  • QLD: Driver Authority (BHTX/TAXI/LIMO), mandatory training
  • WA: Passenger Transport Driver Authorisation (PTD-A) required
  • SA: Driver and Operator Accreditation, vehicle max 8 years

DODO Max Driver Requirements

Want to drive for DODO Max? Here's what you need:

Driver Qualifications

  • Hold a valid unrestricted Australian driver's licence (minimum 12 months)
  • Be at least 21 years of age
  • Have an ABN and be GST-registered
  • Pass a comprehensive background check
  • Hold necessary state-specific accreditations
  • Maintain appropriate commercial insurance
  • Complete DODO driver training program

Vehicle Requirements

  • Vehicle must seat 6-7 passengers comfortably
  • No older than 10 years (varies by state)
  • Must be in excellent condition - no damage, clean interior
  • Pass all state vehicle inspections
  • Registered as a Commercial Passenger Vehicle
  • Equipped with proper insurance coverage
  • Air conditioning must be fully functional
  • Ample luggage space required

Eligible Vehicle Types

  • SUVs (Toyota Kluger, Mazda CX-9, etc.)
  • Minivans (Toyota Tarago, Kia Carnival, etc.)
  • Large MPVs (Honda Odyssey, etc.)
  • Premium 7-seater vehicles
